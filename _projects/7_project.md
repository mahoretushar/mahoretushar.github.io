## Overview

The Covid-19 pandemic saw a surge in misinformation, fear-driven content, and negative narratives across media platforms. This project developed an **AI-based analysis system** that automatically identifies positive, constructive content in media streams and surfaces it for broader distribution to support mental health and community resilience.

Registered as a **Copyright** with the Indian Copyright Office on March 8, 2021.

---

## Motivation

- Pandemic-era media was overwhelmingly negative, contributing to anxiety and panic
- Automated curation of positive content at scale is infeasible manually
- Positive information (safety guidelines, recovery stories, community support) has measurable public health value

---

## System Design

- **Content ingestion** — processes articles, posts, and broadcasts from multiple media sources
- **Sentiment & tone classification** — NLP model classifies content across a positivity spectrum
- **Relevance filtering** — ensures content is factually grounded and pandemic-relevant
- **Broadcasting module** — surfaces high-scoring content via a dashboard or notification feed
- **Feedback loop** — user interactions improve the classification model over time

---

## Key Outcomes

- Automated pipeline from content ingestion to positive-content surfacing
- NLP-based tone classification tuned for pandemic-era language
- Deployable as a web dashboard or RSS-style feed
