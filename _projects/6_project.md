## Overview

During the Omicron wave of the Covid-19 pandemic, rapid early detection was critical to containing spread. This project developed an **AI-based smart system** for early detection of Covid-19 and Omicron symptoms, enabling faster identification before clinical testing. Filed as an **Indian Patent** in June 2022.

---

## Motivation

- PCR and rapid antigen tests require access to testing centers
- Symptom onset precedes positive test results by days — AI can flag likely cases early
- Omicron presented distinct symptom patterns compared to earlier variants, requiring updated detection logic

---

## Approach

- **Symptom profiling** — structured intake of reported symptoms (fever, cough, fatigue, loss of smell/taste, body aches, etc.)
- **Variant-aware classification** — model distinguishes likely Covid-19, Omicron, or negative cases based on symptom combinations
- **ML classifier** — trained on symptom-outcome data from published clinical datasets
- **Risk stratification** — outputs a risk level to guide self-isolation vs. clinical referral decisions

---

## Key Outcomes

- Early detection capability before PCR confirmation
- Omicron-specific detection logic based on variant-distinct symptom patterns
- Lightweight tool accessible without specialist hardware
