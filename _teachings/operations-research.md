## Course Overview

Operations Research applies mathematical modelling to optimise complex decision problems in engineering, logistics, and management.

## Topics Covered

- Introduction to OR: modelling, problem formulation
- Linear Programming: graphical method, Simplex method
- Duality and sensitivity analysis
- Transportation problem: NWCM, Vogel's approximation, MODI method
- Assignment problem: Hungarian method
- Network models: CPM, PERT
- Queuing theory: M/M/1, M/M/s models
- Game theory: minimax, saddle points, mixed strategies
- Simulation: Monte Carlo methods
- Decision theory: decision trees, payoff tables
