Courses taught across institutions including ICEM Pune, PCU Pune, SCOET Amravati, DRGITR Amravati, and Great Learning.

Teaching Philosophy

I focus on building intuition before formalism — students should understand why an algorithm works before they see its mathematical proof. Labs are designed to run on standard hardware so no student is blocked by compute access. I believe in learning by doing: every concept should have a corresponding implementation exercise.
